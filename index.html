Who are we?

About us

Team

Who are we? Simply the group of people minding some modern problems. The one that we are working on now is the deafness - the barrier for some people’s interaction. We hope to wipe this obstacle in near future! Our product was created to support moral and ethical relations in society. We want to help people with hearing impairments better contact in society.

Idea

Today, 360 million people in the world are deaf or hard of hearing. The purpose of creating the “Sounce” application, first of all, is to support a moral and ethical attitude in society. It will simplify communication between people with disabilities and personnel of different service sectors.

Goals

Our goal is not only to remove existing boundaries, but also to prevent future ones, “Sounce” will help the younger generation not to feel the difference between people. At the same time, our technology will become the basis for the development of products for interacting with gadgets using gestures.

How it works?

The user captures whose gestures and receives a translation in real time. In the same way, you can enter the words that you want to translate, and Sounce will create a set of pictures or translation footage that describes the introductions.
